Snoop Dogg Arrested In Sweden For Being Too High

American controversial rapper Snoop Dogg was arrested in Sweden for allegedly driving under the influence of illegal drugs.

Famous American rapper Snoop Dogg was arrested by police in Sweden shortly after he gave a concert on Saturday night.

Daniel Nilsson, the police spokesman, informed the press that “police carrying out roadside controls noticed that Snoop Dogg, whose car was pulled over, seemed to be under the influence of narcotics. He was arrested and taken to the police station to take a urine test.”
