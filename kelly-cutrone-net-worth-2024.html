Early Life and Career Beginnings

Kelly Cutrone was born in Camillus, New York. She moved to New York City in 1987 to pursue a career in public relations. Cutrone started her career at Susan Blond Inc., a PR firm, where she worked for several years before launching her own company, Cutrone & Weinberg.

In 1996, Cutrone founded People’s Revolution, a PR, branding, and marketing firm. The company quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach to public relations and marketing, working with high-profile clients in the fashion industry.
