How can I activate Paytm account without KYC?

How to Activate Paytm Wallet?

  • Install and sign in to the Paytm app on your mobile device.
  • Locate the 'Paytm wallet' option.
  • The Paytm wallet can be activated simply by entering your PAN details/driving license/Aadhaar card number.
  • Start using the wallet to perform all sorts of transactions!
