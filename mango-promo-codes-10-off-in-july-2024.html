Does Mango have free shipping?

Mango has free US shipping on orders of $50 or over. If your order is less than $50, there is a $5.95 shipping charge. Your shipping will be calculated at the checkout with no Mango promo code required. 

When are the Mango sales?

Mango has sales throughout the year on its sale pages. We’ve seen previous discounts of up to 70% off in the sale. Whether you’re shopping for dresses or jeans, you can shop for less with the sale prices.  Alternatively, have a browse of our page to find a Mango coupon code. 

How do I get a 10% Mango coupon code?

Sign up for the Mango newsletter and you will be sent a 10% off Mango discount code to use on your first order. You’ll be kept updated on the latest promotions, exclusive sales, and new styles. Simply visit the Mango website and type your preferred email address into the sign-up box in the corner of your screen. 

Does Mango do student discounts?

Unfortunately, Mango does not have student discounts. If this changes in the future, we’ll let you know. Students can still save today on their orders with our promo codes or by shopping the sale.  

What is the return policy at Mango?

Mango has free returns within 30 days for online purchases. You will need to return your order via UPS or in-store and complete a returns form. Your items should be in the same condition as you bought them. Fragrances, candles, and cosmetics should still have their original label seals. Make sure to double-check the return policy on the Mango website for exclusions.
