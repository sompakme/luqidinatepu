10 stunning Brit Awards after party dresses that we couldn't take our eyes off

We can't decide if we prefer their red carpet or evening looks!

    Celebrities at the 2020 Brit Awards pulled out all the stops on Tuesday night as they arrived at The O2, but if you thought you had your fill of fashion inspiration then you're sorely mistaken! As with all big awards shows, the Brit celebrations did not end there. The likes of Maya Jama, Mel C and Celeste headed on to after parties where they changed into yet another show-stopping outfit.     Alex Scott

    Alex was stunning in a black velvet mini dress with square shoulders and long sleeves, which she teamed with edgy patent heels.

    Kendall Jenner

    Kendall can pull off most outfits and colours, and she had no trouble rocking a lime green jumpsuit and metallic accessories on Tuesday night.

    Mel C

    Spice Girl Mel C certainly knows how to make a fashion statement! She rocked a white and yellow suit layered over a black crop top that showed off her toned abs.

    Ellie Goulding

    While Ellie rocked a colourful Koche gown for the red carpet, she later opted for a simple black glittery number with a low neckline.

    Maya Jama

    Wow! If like us, you were in love with Maya's black velvet gown then you're in for a treat when you see her blue and white evening dress. She dazzled in a sparkly mini depicting clouds and cupids alongside fabulous feather shoes.

    Celeste

    Glittering in green! Celeste changed out of her Gucci cream and silver gown and into a green strapless dress covered in sequins. To finish off her evening look, she added a tiered silver necklace and a sparkly silver bag.

    Irina Shayk

    Taking a daytime look into the evening, Irina wowed in an oversized blue shirt embellished with silver crystals and matching blue sparkly heels. 

    Anne-Sophie Flury

    Anne cosied up to boyfriend, Capital FM presenter Roman Kemp wearing a strappy lilac dress with silver sparkles fading from top to bottom. Such a stylish couple!

    Mabel

    Red was the colour of the evening for Mabel, who swapped her red floor-length Moschino dress for a backless pink and red mini with a tasselled skirt.

    Tallia Storm

    We need Tallia Storm's sparkly blazer! She was pictured at the Universal Music Brit Awards after party in the jazzy blazer with black trousers and chunky heels with an animal print platform heel. 

    Adwoa Aboah

    Model Adwoa looked every inch the model at the Warner Music & CIROC Brit Awards house party, wearing a white slip dress and carrying a black, feathered bag.
