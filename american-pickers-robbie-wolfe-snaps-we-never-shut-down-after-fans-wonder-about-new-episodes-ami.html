American Pickers Robbie Wolfe snaps 'we never shut down' after fans wonder about new episodes ami

ROBBIE Wolfe has clapped back at a fan who asked when new episodes of American Pickers will be returning.

Robbie, 62, is the older brother of Mike Wolfe, 58, who was an original cast member of American Pickers.

Now, the two brothers work together as co-hosts on the show, but new episodes haven't been released since March.

Robbie took to Instagram to show off some of the new antiques that will be for sale online on the picker's website.

He started the video by saying, "Hey guys, just got back from a long trip and we actually just had the pick drops, man.

"This is all stuff we picked on the show."

Some of the items that will be sold include an antique Kellogs sign, Ford car seats that were used in old dealerships, and a ton of other signs - which are a favorite in the picker community.

Robbie said, "All this stuff we're going to be putting up on the website in the next few weeks. So, you know, jump on there."

He captioned the post: "Fresh antiques going up on the website soon!"

In the comment section, a fan asked: "Rob is this a new video? If so how come we're not seeing any of the shows on the History Channel? Thank you."

TOUGH WATERS AHEAD

Though American Pickers has a strong fanbase, the show's ratings haven't reflected that.

After months of plummeting ratings, the show didn't air a new episode for weeks in October 2022.

As cast and crew members took on other projects, there were growing clues the show will soon be canceled.

The original premise of American Pickers followed Frank and his former best friend, Mike, as they traveled across the country looking for rare artifacts and antiques for their shops.

But Frank was fired from the reality series in July 2021 after taking time away to recover from a brutal back surgery that left him with 185 stitches and two rods in his spine.

Frank and Mike also became embroiled in a bitter personal rivalry.
