Rebecca Romijn Net Worth

Rebecca Romijn is an American actress born on November 6, 1972 in Berkeley, CA. She is best known for her role as the shapeshifting mutant Mystique in the X-Men film franchise. She has also had notable roles in the TV series Ugly Betty, the films Lake City, The Punisher, and Rollerball.
