Stéphan Kareem El Shaarawy (born October 27, 1992) is an Egyptian-born Italian professional footballer who now plays as a striker for Serie A club Milan and the national side of his home country, Egypt. Il Faraone is the nickname given to him (The Pharaoh). He began his professional career with Genoa, and following a successful loan spell with Padova, he was acquired by AC Milan in 201During the 2012 season, The Guardian ranked El Shaarawy 52nd in their list of the world’s top 100 footballers. Italian international El Shaarawy has played for the national team as well as the Italy U21 national team. He has also represented the national team’s youth teams, the U17 national team and the U19 national team. In August 2012, he made his senior international debut against England in a friendly match, and three months later, he scored his first international goal against France in a friendly match in November 2012.
