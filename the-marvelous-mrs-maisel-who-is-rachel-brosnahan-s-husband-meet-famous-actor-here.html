The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel: Who is Rachel Brosnahan's husband? Meet famous actor here

The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel recently returned for its fifth and final season much to the delight of fans. Although the plot is what makes the show so brilliant, the fans have come to know and adore Rachel Brosnahan as Miriam 'Midge' Maisel over its many episodes.

Away from her stint as the aspiring stand-up comic, fans are often wanting to know more about her personal life, including her marriage to her famous husband who also has appeared in the popular drama series. Get to know more about Rachel and her marriage to Jason Ralph below…

Who is Rachel Brosnahan's famous husband?

Rachel Brosnahan's husband is actor Jason Ralph who fans will recognize from his stint as Mike Carr in The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel. Jason began appearing in the show from season four onwards – but the pair were romantically involved long before he joined his wife in the cast.

The couple met on the set of the movie, I'm Obsessed With You, which was filmed in 2013. Despite both being in the cast and getting to know one another, they didn't begin dating until after filming stopped. However, in 2015, Rachel and Jason then made their first public appearance together as a couple at a theatre event in New York City.

In September 2018, the pair attended the Emmy Awards together and sparked rumours of a marriage when they were spotted wearing matching bands on their ring fingers. It was reported by PEOPLE that they had tied the knot earlier that year, but in 2019, Rachel told the SAG Awards that she and Jason had actually "been married for years."

What has Rachel Brosnahan said about her marriage?
