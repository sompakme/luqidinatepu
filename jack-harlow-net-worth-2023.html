Jack Harlow Net Worth 2023

According to financial reports, Harlow’s net worth is estimated to be around $5 million as of 2023.

The 25-year-old Louisville, Kentucky native first hit the scene in 2020 with his major label debut album “That’s What They All Say”, which peaked at number 5 on the Billboard 200 chart.

Powered by the smash single “What’s Poppin” and its TikTok fame, the album propelled Harlow into the mainstream and earned him his first Grammy nomination for Best Rap Performance.

Additionally, Harlow brings in revenue from touring, feature verses, YouTube advertising, merchandise sales, and other commercial partnerships with brands like KFC, New Balance, and Call of Duty. His 2023 “Come Home The Kids Miss You Tour” sold out dates across the U.S. and Europe, likely earning him a substantial payday.

Forbes estimates that between June 2021-2022, Harlow earned around $9 million from his various music, streaming, and business ventures.

In 2023, they project he could accumulate as much as $13 million with the continued success of singles like “First Class” and “Poison” dominating charts and playlists after the release of his second album.

So at just 24 years old, Jack Harlow has already built an impressive net worth that looks to grow substantially in the coming years as he cements himself as a major force in today’s hip-hop landscape. His witty punchlines, catchy tracks, and marketable persona make him one valuable rising superstar.
