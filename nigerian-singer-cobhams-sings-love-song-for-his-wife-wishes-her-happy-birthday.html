Nigerian singer Cobhams sings love song for his wife, wishes her happy birthday

- Nigerian singer Cobhams Asuquo has celebrated his beautiful wife on her birthday

- The singer sang a love song and penned a sweet birthday message to his wife

- Asuquo expressed that it was only his wife that could make him do something as romantic as this

Popular Nigerian singer Cobhams Asuquo has celebrated his beautiful wife, Ojuola, in a romantic way. The singer put his angelic voice into a romantic use to sing a beautiful love song for his wife.

The singer shared a video of himself singing a love song for his wife on her birthday. Cobhams shared the video on his Instagram page.

In the video, the singer was heard singing his own version of an old song known as Mo Le Fi 1000 Fe.
