Byron Bay entrepreneurs list $7.2m luxury Gold Coast pad

The entrepreneurial Byron Bay couple Tom and Emma Lane have ventured over the border into South East Queensland, but it seems they don’t plan on staying too long.

The couple, who’ve recently heralded their move to the NSW Southern Highlands, have listed their newly completed apartment at 272 Hedges Avenue, Mermaid Beach.

The Lanes paid $7.2m for a 513sq m, two-level sky home with a 132sq m wrap around balcony.
