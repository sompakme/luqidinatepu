Pax Jolie-Pitt once blasted despicable dad Brad Pitt as world class a**hole

Pax Jolie-Pitt reportedly blasted his estranged father, Brad Pitt, as a “world class a**hole” in an Instagram post on Father’s Day 2020.

The then 16-year-old used his private account to slam the Once Upon a Time in Hollywood star four years after Pitt allegedly abused both his ex-wife Angelina Jolie and two of their six children aboard a private jet.

“Happy Father’s Day to this world class a**hole!! You time and time again prove yourself to be a terrible and despicable person,” began the message, which theDaily Mail published Monday.

“You have no consideration or empathy toward your 4 youngest children who tremble in fear when in your presence,” Pax, now 19, reportedly continued, referring to Zahara, 18, Shiloh, 17, and twins Vivienne and Knox, 15 (Pax’s older brother, Maddox, is 22.)

The college student, who purportedly penned the message over a photo of Pitt accepting an Oscar in 2020, wrote that the Fight Club star would “never understand the damage” he inflicted on their family because he is “incapable of doing so.”

“You have made the lives of those closest to me a constant hell,” he continued, according to the report. “You may tell yourself and the world whatever you want, but the truth will come to light someday.

“So, Happy Father’s Day, you f**king awful human being!!!”

Reps for Pitt and Jolie didn’t immediately return Page Six’s requests for comment.

The actor, 59, has not been photographed with any of his children since 2016 when the “Tomb Raider” star, 48, filed for divorce following the highly publicised plane ordeal.

The FBI investigated Pitt for the alleged child abuse but decided not to pursue criminal charges.

In October 2022, Jolie claimed in court documents that her kids were “traumatised” by her ex-husband’s alleged behaviour, which took place when he was inebriated.

The Se7en actor allegedly “choked one of the children and struck another in the face,” as well as “grabbed Jolie by the head and shook her,” the filing stated.

“At one point, he poured beer on Jolie; at another, he poured beer and red wine on the children.”

A source close to Pitt told us at the time that the Maleficent star’s claims were “completely untrue.”

Although Pax’s rant took place more than three years ago, Zahara appeared to shade Pitt as recently as last week when she introduced herself as “Zahara Marley Jolie” and left off “Pitt” during her sorority induction.
