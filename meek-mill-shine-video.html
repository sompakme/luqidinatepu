Meek Mill &quot;Shine&quot; Video

With DC4, Meek Mill may not have erased all that went down between him and Drake last summer, but with all the hype around the release, he certainly reasserted his place in the top tier of the rap game. Only Meek's staunchest of foes can deny the visceral energy of some of the tape's tracks, including "Shine," which is proving to be an early favorite. The spirit -- one of pure inspiration -- is in the vein of the classic "Dreams and Nightmares Intro." "Shine" has now become the first DC4 track to receive a music video. Shot in black and white, the new visual shows Meek living the dream with all of the Dreamchasers he's been with since the bottom. After "Shine" plays out, the color switches back on, and Meek raps part of "Blessed Up," another DC4 solo track. Both "Shine" and "Blessed Up" are produced by the duo of StreetRunner and Tarik Azzouz.
