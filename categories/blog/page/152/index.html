Do dentures change face shape?

published on August 31, 2024
The short answer is yes, dentures can definitely change the shape of your face. But if you've been missing teeth or dealing with bad, ill-fitting dentures, the change may be for the better. How does your face change with dentures? When you're wearing dentures, your jawbone can lose volume, including in the chin area.
