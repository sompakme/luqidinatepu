WHAT HAPPENED TO MICHAEL AND CHRISTINE GACY?

Michael and Christine Gacy were confronted with a terrifying reality when their father, serial killer John Wayne Gacy, was convicted of murdering 33 boys and men between 1972 and 1978, dressed in a clown suit and makeup. His children — and society at large — were unaware that Gacy had buried his victims' bodies under his house.
