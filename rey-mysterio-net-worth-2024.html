Early Life and Career Beginnings

Rey Mysterio was born and raised in Chula Vista, California. He was trained by his uncle, Rey Misterio Sr., a professional wrestler. Mysterio made his wrestling debut in Mexico at the age of 14. His early career was marked by his performances in the Mexican wrestling promotion, Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA).

Major Wrestling Promotions

Mysterio’s career took off when he joined Extreme Championship Wrestling (ECW) in 1995. He later moved to World Championship Wrestling (WCW), where he became a well-known figure in the cruiserweight division. In 2001, Mysterio joined World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), where he won numerous championships and became one of the company’s most popular superstars.
