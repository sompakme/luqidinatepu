What happened to Darcelle XV? Oldest Drag Queen Walter W. Cole dies at 92

On March 23, 2023, the world lost a beloved entertainer and icon, Walter W. Cole, who was better known as Darcelle XV. He was 92 years old at the time of his passing. Darcelle was a renowned American drag queen, entertainer, and the owner and operator of Darcelle XV Showplace, a cabaret nightclub located in Portland, Oregon. Guinness World Records had certified him as the oldest drag queen performer in 2016, with a career as an entertainer spanning 57 years at the time of his death.

Darcelle entertained generations of tourists and locals alike, particularly bachelorette parties, for over 50 years at his Old Town nightclub. The club was an institution in the city, and Darcelle was a beloved figure in the LGBTQ+ community. He was also a civic icon, known for his philanthropy and community service.
