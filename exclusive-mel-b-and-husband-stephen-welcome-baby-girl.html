Exclusive: Mel B and husband Stephen welcome baby girl

    Spice Girl Melanie Brown is celebrating after giving birth to a baby girl, hellomagazine.com can exclusively reveal. The new arrival is a sister for her two other girls 12-year-old Phoenix Chi and four year old Angel.

    She was born in the Cedars-Sinai Hospital, where ex-Spice Girl colleague Victoria Beckham gave birth to Harper Seven last July. "Finally our baby arrives, Stephen nearly passed out, Phoenix screamed, I laughed so hard the baby popped out!!! She is just sooo amazing!!" tweeted Mel soon after the birth.The delighted couple also released a statement: "This evening Melanie Brown and husband Stephen Belafonte welcomed their new baby girl in Los Angeles. "This is the first child for Mel and Stephen and both mum and baby are happy, healthy and doing well. "The former Spice Girl's colleagues were quick to send in their congratulations too. "Yeah!!! Well done Mummy! Lots of love. Xxx," wrote Emma Bunton. "Congrats So happy for ya All , See ya soon In Oz for #xfactorau," tweeted her fellow Australian judge Ronan Keating.At Mel's recent baby shower she exclusively told HELLO! Magazine: "I can't wait to meet this baby. "The whole family is beside themselves. Both Phoenix and Angel arrived six weeks early. I've never been this pregnant before."

    "Yep thats right i am over due with this baby, and this is a first!!!," she tweeted this week.

    "The baby will come when it's good and ready, so for now working as usual to keep my mind off being over due!!, " she added.

    "Phoenix and Angel will be there with me during my labour, which is just as well as Stephen will be a complete nightmare. "He'll probably pass out and require medical attention," she confessed to HELLO!.Mel hasn't revealed the name of Phoenix and Angel's little sister yet.
