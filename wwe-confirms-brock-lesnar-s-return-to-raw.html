WWE Confirms Brock Lesnars Return To RAW

Brock Lesnar always bring attention to himself when he shows up on WWE television. The last time he showed up on WWE RAW, he almost tore Cody Rhodes apart. Fans won’t have to wait long to see him again.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE RAW this week.

Brock Lesnar was not around at WWE RAW this week. Cody Rhodes was there by himself and cut a promo on the Beast Incarnate before facing Finn Balor later on in the night.

Before WWE RAW went off the air this week, they confirmed that Brock Lesnar will be back on RAW next week. It was not noted what he will be doing, but Lesnar will be around.

Next week’s episode of WWE RAW will mark the 2nd night of the 2023 WWE Draft. Only time will tell which brand gets Brock Lesnar when the dust settles, but his target will likely be on Cody Rhodes until they collide at Backlash on May 6th.

Brock Lesnar wasn’t around this week on RAW, but there was a big surprise backstage. As we previously reported, CM Punk was asked to leave WWE RAW this week, but he was backstage for while. Punk reportedly got to see several members of the roster and also talk to Triple H. It was also reported exactly who asked CM Punk to leave. Some WWE Superstars also believe that CM Punk only showed up to hype his AEW return.
