Yeezy Desert Boots Surface In Multiple Colorways

The updated Yeezy Boots will reportedly come in several different colorways such as "Graphite Suede" and "Taupe," as well as "Forest" and "Blue Steel." All four of the tonal boots appear to feature a combination of suede, mesh and nubuck with a chunky outsole and an equally puffy tongue that is strapped down by thick rope laces.

According to Yeezy Mafia, the Yeezy Season 6 boots in "Graphite Suede" and "Taupe" will retail for $270, while the "Forest" and "Blue Steel" Yeezy Season 7 joints will cost $225. All four boots look identical so it's unclear why some are cheaper than the others. Either way, the price tags are much more consumer-friendly than the original Yeezy military boots that retailed for more than $400.
