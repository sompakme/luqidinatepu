Celebrity Twin Moments: Wizkid and American Rapper Roddy Ricch Rock N4.3m Jacket

  • Grammy-winning singer, Wizkid, recently stepped out rocking a designer jacket worth millions of naira

Photos of the two stars have been put in a collage with fans sharing their thoughts on who rocked it better.
