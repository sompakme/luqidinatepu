Versatile NBA forward who was drafted 35th overall by the Golden State Warriors in 2012. He helped the Warriors win NBA Championships in 2015, 2017 and 2018. He was named to his first All-Star game in 2016 and in 2017, he was named NBA Defensive Player of the Year.
