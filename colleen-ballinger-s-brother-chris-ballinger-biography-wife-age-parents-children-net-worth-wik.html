Career

Chris Ballinger is a popular YouTube content creator best known for his channel “Ballinger Family,” which he started on January 7, 2013.

Over the years, he has gained a large following, with one of his most popular videos, “The Jennifer Lawrence Magic Trick,” uploaded in 2014.

In September of that same year, Chris collaborated with fellow YouTuber JoshuaDTV on the “Improv Duet Challenge” video, showcasing his talent for entertaining and engaging content.

Chris Ballinger discovered his passion for magic during college and honed his skills by performing on the streets of Pasadena to earn money.

As an Academy of Magical Arts member, he gained more experience by performing at restaurants and birthday parties.

He also worked as a production assistant for the TV series Bones at Fox Studios. Before gaining notoriety, Chris made over 1,000 YouTube videos from his studio apartment in Santa Barbara, all under the title “Magic Geek” on the website Juggling Store.

Aside from his successful career as a magician and YouTuber, Chris Ballinger has also played a significant role in his sister Colleen’s rise to fame.

He co-authored a bestselling book with her, “Self Help,” and has helped to write scripts for her popular YouTube channel, Miranda Sings.

In 2015, he landed a deal with Netflix to co-write an 8-episode series based on Colleen’s “Miranda Sings” character, “Haters Back Off.” Chris has been a valuable collaborator and support system for his sister’s success.
