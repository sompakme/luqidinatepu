Details You Didn't Know About Olivia Palermo

Olivia Palermo first came onto the scene in 2006 as a budding New York socialite who got invited to New York Fashion week after-parties for Zac Posen and Calvin Klein, which eventually landed in the pages of Vogue, according to Women's Wear Daily. During her stint on The City from 2008-2010, Palermo began focusing on building her own brand and fashion line while modeling. She was a consultant for the Gap-owned e-retailer Piperlime, served as a guest judge for Britain and Ireland's Next Top Model, and was named creative director of the British cosmetics brand Ciat√©, according to the Observer.
