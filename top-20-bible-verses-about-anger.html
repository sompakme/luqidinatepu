Anger is, may be the most destructive, intense and unhealthy emotion; it may lead to terrible consequences. As you see, Bible anger quotes can be divided into two main categories. The verses of the first category describe the cases of righteous anger, this type of feeling appears in God when He wants to punish the sinners or when the hearts of people are turned out away from Him.

The second type is unrighteous anger; this is the feeling of people that often leads to committing sins. The Bible gives as a great antidote against this type of feeling, and this antidote is forgiveness. The wisdom and an ability to forgive — these are the main methods to avoid the sin of rage.

Bible verses about anger help us to understand all the main aspects of this feeling or state. The scripture gives us the methods to overcome destructive rage, so let us avoid any sinful states.
