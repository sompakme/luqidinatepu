Plotlines and Ratings

Weak Hero is a webtoon series that follows the story of Yeon Shi Eun, a small and introverted high school student who fights against the bullies and tyrants in his school. He is not a typical hero who relies on physical strength or special abilities, but a smart and cunning one who uses his intelligence and strategy to overcome his opponents.

He also forms alliances and friendships with other students who share his ideals and values, such as Ben Park, Eugene Gale, and Alex Go. Together, they challenge the Big Four, the four strongest and most feared fighters in the city, who rule the school with an iron fist. The webtoon explores the themes of violence, bullying, friendship, loyalty, justice, and courage, as well as the dark and realistic side of high school life.
