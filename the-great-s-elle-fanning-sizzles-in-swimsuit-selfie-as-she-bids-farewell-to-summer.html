The Great's Elle Fanning sizzles in swimsuit selfie as she bids farewell to summer

Summer might be coming to an end but Elle Fanning made sure she went out with a bang and shared a stunning swimsuit selfie to bid the season farewell.

The Great actress posted a sun-drenched snapshot to her Instagram stories over the Labor Day weekend and wrote: "I guess Summer is over," along with a crying emoji.
