What is better over or under breast implants?

The under the muscle or submuscular approach involves placing the implants below the chest muscle. This is usually a good option for women with little native breast tissue, as the muscle offers greater coverage. The results tend to look more natural than breasts that are placed above the muscle.

What implants feel the most natural?

Silicone breast implants tend to feel more like natural breast tissue than saline alternatives. This is particularly true of the form stable (“gummy bear”) breast implant, which is filled with a cohesive gel that mimics the feel of natural breast tissue almost perfectly.

Do breast implants get moldy?

It's widely thought that saline implants are harmless when they leak. However, saline implants are prone to mold, yeast and bacteria growth. Deterioration or damage to the implant's seal allows bacteria to grow inside the implant. This fluid can slowly leak out of the implant and spread throughout the body.

How painful is breast augmentation recovery?

Your Recovery

After the surgery you will probably feel weak. You may feel sore for 2 to 3 weeks, and you'll likely have a lot of swelling. You may have a pulling or stretching feeling in your breast area. You can expect to feel better and stronger each day, although you may need pain medicine for a week or two.
