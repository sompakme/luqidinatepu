Penelope Cruz Net Worth

Penelope Cruz is a Spanish actress born on April 28, 1974 in Alcobendas, Spain. She is best known for her Academy Award-winning performance in the 2008 film Vicky Cristina Barcelona. She has also starred in other films such as Volver, Vanilla Sky, Blow, Zoolander 2, and All the Pretty Horses.

About

Spanish Actress who won the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for her role as Maria Elena in the 2008 film Vicky Cristina Barcelona. She also played leading roles in the films Volver, Vanilla Sky, Blow, Zoolander 2 and All the Pretty Horses. 

Before Fame

She studied classical ballet for nine years, and signed with an acting agent when she was 15. 

Trivia

She served as a spokesmodel fro L'Oreal and also modeled for Ralph Lauren. 

Family Life

She dated Tom Cruise and Matthew McConaughey before marrying Javier Bardem in 2010. She and Javier had a son named Leo and a daughter named Luna. 

Associated With

She appeared in the Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides with Johnny Depp in 2011.
