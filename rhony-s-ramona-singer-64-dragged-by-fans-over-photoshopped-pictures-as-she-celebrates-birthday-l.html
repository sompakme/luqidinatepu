RHONY's Ramona Singer, 64, dragged by fans over 'photoshopped' pictures as she celebrates birthday l

REAL Housewives of New York's Ramona Singer, 64, is dragged by her fans over "photoshopped" and "filtered" pictures as she celebrates her birthday looking half her age.

The criticism comes from her most recent Instagram post, a selfie snapped with Housewives co-star Luann de Lesseps.

The pair smiled while holding a sugar and fruit-coated dessert with a pink candle, in celebration of Ramona's 64th birthday.

The caption reads: "Woke up early to meet @countessluann for coffee and she surprised me with a little birthday cake with a candle! I love our friendship! Thank you, Luann!! & Thank you to everyone for all of the birthday love today!! Xo."

Fans were quick to head to the comments section to share their thoughts on the Real Housewives of New York's birthday post.

Commenters were shocked at Ramona's appearance, claiming she looked half her age and "photoshopped."

They dragged her for getting a "facelift" and begged her to stop filtering her face so heavily.

One fan even went so far to say that "everyone [has] ironed their faces."

This isn't the first time Ramona has been slammed for her blatant photo editing.

Last week, the reality star shared an almost unrecognizable photo of herself while getting her hair styled.

Ramona's followers were fascinated to know which editing apps she uses to appear so young, and some asked for a "natural photo, no filter."

However, the TV personality clapped back, saying: "No filter app, it's my makeup artist's digital camera."

Although some fans were supportive of her pursuit of youth, others find the encouragement toxic, with comments like: "Why are you all encouraging this by telling her she looks great? It's unrealistic and can cause real damage."

Other photos have been targeted from online haters as well, and one posted by Housewives co-star Sonja Morgan had fans shocked and concerned about Ramona's "new" face.

Countless people asked: "What is wrong with Ramona’s face?”

One voiced: “Ramona should stop messing with her face" while others swore the mother of one had gotten a "facelift."

The Bravo star has previously admitted to having cosmetic surgeries, in 2016 acknowledging the fact that she's gotten botox several times.

The blonde says she gets the enhancement a few times a year on her forehead, around her eyes, as well as an eye lift and fillers.

She's also previously confessed to getting breast implants, saying: “I am over 50, let’s face it, gravity hits. It wasn’t hitting that badly, but I wanted to look perkier. 

“I wanted to be perky the way I was in my 30’s."

But the housewife shuts down any claims that she has had recent work done, stating: “Why would I have anything planned? I look the best I’ve ever looked. I think I look great. I’m very happy."
