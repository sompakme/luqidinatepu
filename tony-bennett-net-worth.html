Early Life and Education

Tony Bennett was born on August 3, 1926, in Astoria, Queens, New York City. His parents were immigrants from Calabria, Italy. Bennett started singing at a very young age, and when he was ten years old, he performed with his brother in front of relatives and friends at a church in New Jersey. He attended the High School of Industrial Arts in New York, and after his father died, he took up a job as a singing waiter to help support his family.

Career

Tony Bennett’s career as a singer began in the 1950s. He gained his first commercial success in 1951 with his single “Because of You,” and soon after, he signed a contract with Columbia Records. Personal Life

Legacy
