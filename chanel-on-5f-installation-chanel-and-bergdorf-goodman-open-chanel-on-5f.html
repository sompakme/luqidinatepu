Chanel on 5F Installation - Chanel and Bergdorf Goodman Open Chanel on 5F

Get thee to the gym. The house of Chanel has unveiled it's latest installation on the 5th floor of Bergdorf Goodman's, a reinterpreted sports club inspired by the Fall 2014 ready-to-wear ad campaign 'Coco Coach.' Customers can shop the sportif collection from locker room to bench press in true technical fashion. Highlights from the installation are also on display on the 57th Street windows—a first time the famed Bergdorf windows have been dedicated to one brand—again proving the power of the mighty Karl Lagerfeld.

Bergdorf's feted the opeing with a cocktail party last night. CHANEL on 5F will be open through Sunday, October 19.
