13 Unique Winter Wonderland Party Ideas For Adults

Take your winter wonderland party to the mountains! A venue like this Connecticut cabin not only offers beautiful winter vibes but plenty of winter wonderland party ideas for adults built in. For example, why not go ice skating, snowshoeing, cross-country skiing, or ice fishing during your stay!

Never cross-country skied? This party is your chance! Look for Peerspace venues that border on National Forests or have enough private land to tromp around and try a new winter sport! This mountain home in Washington has both. Not only does it come with three acres to try out new activities on, but it is also adjacent to the Wenatchee National Forest with varied terrain that includes a mature forest, fields, and rolling hills.

The best dessert for a winter wonderland party is s’mores, of course! S’mores are a great way to combine a fun activity with yummy treats and plentiful conversation with friends. Many Peerspaces, like this Stanhope home, come with fire pits and bonfire spaces. In fact, you can even use the “more filters” button on Peerspace and select “fire pit” as an amenity requirement for your Peerspace search!

Candles are a decor item that can immediately give a space cozy, winter feels! By adding candles, your winter wonderland space will quickly come to life. For instance, check out how the candles make this New York City event venue pop!

The true winter wonderland is on the slopes! While booking the entire ski hill for your party may be a bit of a stretch, renting a venue with the ski slopes right outside your front door isn’t! This Washington Peerspace venue is located right at Snoqualmie Pass. Beautiful views and endless party opportunities!

If you are planning a smaller winter wonderland party (or even a more intimate after-party), consider renting a space with a large hot tub! Hot tubbing is a great activity during a winter wonderland party that features cooler temperatures. Even better is a space like this estate in Texas that not only comes with a hot tub but also a swim spa, pool, barbecue area, and game room!

A polar plunge isn’t an activity for the faint of heart! During your party, ask your guests to strip down and run into the freezing cold water. This winter wonderland party idea for adults is ideal if you have rented a Peerspace with lake access or even a large pond like at this Massachusetts lakefront home.
