Production Career and Movies

Gaurang Doshi shared that he received production training from his father. Also, he took training under the guidance of the famous film producer J.P. Dutta in the movies Yateem and Hathyar. Also, he took knowledge from the director duo Abbas Mustan. In 2000, he produced his first movie titled ‘Bawandar’, which is based on the real story of Bhanwari Devi. She was a rape victim from Rajasthan. Afterward, he decided to produce another film titled ‘Aankhen’.

He somehow managed to talk with the legendary actor named Amitabh Bachchan and convinced him to play a Negative role in his film. The movie was a blockbuster and became the second-highest-grosser film in the year 2002. This movie had its worldwide premiere in Malaysia. Afterward, he produced ‘Deewaar, Let’s Bring Our Hero’s Home’ which was also a major hit. The star cast of the film was Sanjay Dutt, Akshaye Khanna, Amitabh Bachchan, Amrita Rao, and more.

Gaurang Doshi is the owner of Gaurang Doshi Productions Private Limited. Also, he is the founder of Tiger Trail Production and Maisha Infotech Pvt. Ltd. As per his official website, he is looking for other projects such as Aankhen Returns, Indian In Danger, Line of Fire, Happy Anniversary, and more.
