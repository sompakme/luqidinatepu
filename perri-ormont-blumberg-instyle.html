Experience

Perri Ormont Blumberg is a New York City-born-and-based writer. She holds a B.A. in psychology from Columbia University and is also a culinary school graduate from the Natural Gourmet Institute. Her writing has appeared in publications such as the New York Post, The Wall Street Journal, Travel + Leisure, Business Insider, Men’s Journal, TODAY.com, BestProducts.com, Architectural Digest, Bon Appétit, Fox News, Robb Report, Men’s Health, Martha Stewart, SFgate.com, and many others. Previously, she was a senior staff writer at Southern Living, news editor at Men's Journal, and food editor at Reader's Digest.
