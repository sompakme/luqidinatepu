He Came to Me 3 Days Ago &amp; Said He Will Fight for Himself: Late Mohbads Mum Says She Is Exh

  • Late Mohbad's mother has made some revelations about her deceased son and some of the things he told her
  • In a recent interview, the woman mentioned that she often sees her late son, and he always appears to be in a sad mood
  • She mentioned that the late singer promised her he would fight for himself and she told him to go ahead for she was exhausted

Abosede Aloba, the mother of the late singer, Ilerioluwa Aloba, better known as Mohbad, seemed not done talking about her ordeal since she lost her son last year.

Legit.ng had reported that Mohbad died in his prime at the age of 27 years. A lot of controversies surfaced after his unfortunate death as his family was divided over DNA test and his burial.

In a clip of her interview with Punch Newspaper, the middle aged woman shared some of the things she has been going through since she lost her son.

According to her, she thinks about her son often and sees him in a sad mood. She also said that the late singer told her he would fight for himself.

Late Mohbad's mum says she is exhausted

In the video, Aloba called on Nigerians to help so she could bury her son as she added that she was exhausted.

She explained that many people have been raining curses on her and making her go through hell.

Late Mohbad's mother talks about her brother

In the clip, she mentioned that her brother Darosa, who was late Mohbad's assistant, had not spoken to her since the singer died.

Aloba noted that the man was angry as she called on him to come out and say what he knew about her son's unfortunate passing.

Recall that late Mohbad's mother had earlier rained curses on the killers of her son. She said they will die like Judas Iscariot.

She said:

"I think about my child every day and I see him like a human being sometimes when I look back. He would bow his head down. He came to meet me three days ago and told me he would fight. I am tired of the curses. Though I am not afraid because I have done nothing, They cursed my mother who did not know anything about the case. They have cursed my brother Darosa too."
