Kaley Cuoco kicks off The Big Bang Theory filming with new bob hairstyle

    As Kaley Cuoco prepares to film the latest season of hit show , the actress has returned to the set with a bold new look. The actress debuted her new look on Instagram, revealing that she had inches chopped off her long wavy locks into a shoulder-length bob hairstyle.

    Kaley Cuoco has debuted a new long bob hairstyle

    The 30-year-old flaunted her makeover in the striking snap, which sees her posing in front of a mirror, her hair left down in loose, tousled waves. "Sending mad [heart emoji] and props to @faye.woods for bringing my cut and colour back to its 'Penny' roots #nofilter," she wrote in the snap's accompanying caption for her 2.8 million followers.

    Meanwhile, celebrity stylist Faye Woods also took to Instagram and shared Kaley's photo, revealing in the caption that she had opted for a balayage technique to achieve the star's sunkissed colour.

    It's been a busy few days for the star, with the filming for the tenth season of having started this week. Kaley shared her excitement at being reunited with her fellow cast members, posting a series of sweet photos of the group on Instagram. 

    Kaley posted a series of sweet group photos on set 

    One photo sees them all posing for a selfie together, beaming for the camera and holding up ten fingers each to celebrate the show's ongoing success. "#season10 woot woot!" Kaley captioned the photo. "@thebigbangtheory__ we back babyyyy #selfiestick #backstagepass."

    It's not the first time that the actress has marked the filming of with a makeover. After finishing the previous season, the actress revealed she had extensions put into her locks for a fresh start to the summer.

    "It's very important for me to change my look after wrapping each season :) I love playing Penny, but it's good for me to bring it back to Kaley," she explained in the photo's caption. "Last year chopped it all off – this year I'm doing the opposite. With the extension master @faye.woods…. Stay tuned!"
