Elaine Welteroth and husband Jonathan Singletary mark exciting milestone

The Talk star tied the knot during the pandemic

    Sunday marked an exciting milestone for co-host Elaine Welteroth and her husband Jonathan Singletary. The couple are eight months into their marriage, with Elaine joking that in pandemic times, it feels like years.

    Taking to Instagram to share a gorgeous selfie of the pair, Elaine wrote: "8 months into marriage (which is basically 5 years if we're quarantine-counting) and there's still no one I'd rather ride around and get it with (aka run Sunday errands) than you @jonathansingletary.

    "Oh, and thanks for searching all over the pitch black beach for my phone last night when I lost it in the sand and then tracking it down at the police station like iPhone Columbo. I couldn't have posted this photo without you #Husbae #aRealOne. HAPPY SUNDAY, FAM! What are y'all doing to decompress?"

    Elaine and her beau tied the knot in May 2020 amid the pandemic, when many couples were forced to postpone their wedding. But nothing could stop Elaine and Jonathan saying "I do" outside their front door in Brooklyn, as the best-selling author later revealed on Instagram: "Some things only come around once in a lifetime. This love. That day. Our moment. Too divinely aligned to let it pass us by. Let this be remembered as the day LOVE. COULD. NOT. BE. CANCELLED. #LoveLockdown."
