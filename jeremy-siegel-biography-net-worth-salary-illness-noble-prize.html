Who is Jeremy Siegel?

Jeremy Siegel is a big shot in the finance world, a professor of finance at the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

What is Jeremy Siegel famous for?

Siegel is known for his groundbreaking book, “Stocks for the Long Run,” which argues that over the long haul, stocks are the best investment you can make.

He’s also a regular on financial news programs and a sought-after speaker at conferences.

How long has Jeremy Siegel been at Wharton?

Siegel has been a professor at Wharton for over 50 years, so you could say he’s a bit of an institution there.

He started teaching at Wharton in 1976 and hasn’t looked back since.

In addition to his teaching duties, he’s also the academic director of the school’s Rodney L. White Center for Financial Research.
