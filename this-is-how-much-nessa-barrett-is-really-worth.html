This Is How Much Nessa Barrett Is Really Worth

Nessa Barrett made her way into the world in August 2002 when she was born in New Jersey. That means that she was still just a teenager when she found fame on TikTok. And if she continues to take advantage of her fame — like releasing music and establishing herself as a singer-songwriter, as well as being an online influencer — then she'll be able to add to the fortune that she has already earned. Speaking of that fortune, it reportedly sits at an impressive $1 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

What does Barrett do with that kind of money? Well, in October 2020, she opened up to Refinery29 for an episode of Spill It to reveal what she carried in her "simple" $2,895 Christian Dior calfskin 2-way backpack. For someone young and rich like Barrett, that includes $75 Donna Born In Roma Eau de Parfum from Valentino that she applies "every hour," which means that she must go through a lot of it!

On the flip side, and perhaps strangely for someone with so much money, Barrett doesn't carry a wallet, although she does keep a few affordable items with her like press-on nails that were less than $6, a $12 velvet scrunchie from Urban Outfitters, and sunglasses that only cost her $14.95. Although she's obviously willing to be frugal in some ways, it's clear by the bag that put her back just under $3,000 that she's willing to spend some serious money whenever she so desires.
