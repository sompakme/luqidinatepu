PHOTOS: Meet Late Prophet Ajanaku's Wife

Meet the Wife of the late founder and spiritual leader of the Christ Revival Victory Chapel International, Pastor Elijah Iretiola Ajanaku.

Her name is Joy Ajanaku. They got married in 2013 but sources say their marriage became legalized in 2010.

She has two children for the late prophet, a girl and a boy. The first is Stella and she is 8 while the second, Apoti Eri is 3. She is in her 30's but they hardly stepped out together.

The Gospel singer, Tope Alabi's estranged spiritual father was said to have passed away on the 19th of August, but was finally confirmed dead when a funeral rite for the late pastor was conducted after claims that he resurrected.
