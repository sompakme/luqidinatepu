Watch One Woman Get A Transformational Brazilian Butt Lift in Miami

If Los Angeles is the plastic surgery capitol of boob jobs, Miami is the epicenter of butts. And nobody knows butts better than Dr. Constantino Mendieta. He's the go-to plastic surgeon in the area for one of the hottest treatments in cosmetic surgery: Brazilian butt lifts.

Brazilian butt lift is just a snazzy marketing name for a procedure that involves meticulous liposuction and fat transfer. Fat is sourced from the lower back, abdomen, and sometimes the arms and thighs, sucked out, then injected into the butt through tiny incisions.
