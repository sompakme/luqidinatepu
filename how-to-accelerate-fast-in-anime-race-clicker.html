How to accelerate fast in anime race clicker?

People are currently reading this guide.
There are many ways to speed up your progress in Anime Race Clicker. You can use buffs such as the Quick Start Super Energy and Energy Bomb which will give you a temporary speed boost to help you gain ground faster. You can also spend your Gold on upgrades like the Engine Rebuild and the Tires Boost which will permanently increase your base speed. Finally you can use time skips like the Time Machine and the Time Portal to skip ahead in the game by a predetermined amount. However be sure to save time skips for when you need them most as they are limited resources.
