Watch: LIFE trailer featuring Ryan Reynolds and Jake Gyllenhaal

Have you ever wondered what life would be like on Mars?

A new chilling sci-fi film trailer has been released answering that question exactly, and it's got a super star cast to boot.

Life tells the story of the six-member crew of the International Space Station on the cutting edge of one of the most important discoveries in human history: the first evidence of extraterrestrial life on Mars.

In typical alien flick style, as the crew begins to conduct research their methods end up having unintended consequences and the life form proves more intelligent than anyone ever expected.

The cast includes Jake Gyllenhaal, Rebecca Ferguson, Ryan Reynolds, Hiroyuki Sanada, Ariyon Bakare and Olga Dihovichnaya.

Life is set to be released in May 2017.
