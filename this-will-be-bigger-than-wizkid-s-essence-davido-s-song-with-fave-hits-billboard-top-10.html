This Will Be Bigger Than Wizkids Essence: Davidos Song With Fave Hits Billboard Top 10,

  • Davido’s new masterpiece, Kante, with emPawa signed artist Fave, is currently making waves on the international scene
  • The new song, which happens to be on the recently released album Timeless, made it to the top 10 on Billboard’s Hot Trending Chart

Davido’s wholesome collabo, Kante, with Afro-pop rising star Fave, made it to the top 10 on the Billboard Hot Trending songs.

The song, which melds the sonic deliveries of both artists, sits in the 7th position on the international song chart.

Davido’s feature with the singer overtook other international pop songs on the chart.
