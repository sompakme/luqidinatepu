A FASHIONISTA has revealed how to make a variety of Halloween costumes using clothes that most people already have in their closets.

She shared the outfit ideas as many people prepare to go to parties and other fun events to embrace the spooky season.

Monika Mancini (@monikkamancini) has garnered over 871,000 followers on TikTok, where she shares fashion advice for women with a fuller bust.

She donned a pair of jeans and a plunging black top as she shared the first of two outfits.

“Last minute Halloween costume ideas with things you probably already have at home because if you’re anything like me you leave everything until the last minute," she said.

“Throw on any little white dress that you own and grab black stockings, preferably fish nets.

“If you own a black corset it would be better but I didn’t so I just used a black belt.

“And the center for this look are knee-high black boots and then tie a scarf around your head it just completes the look.

“Of course, accessorize with gold jewelry.

“And there you have it, a cute sexy pirate that literally took you two minutes.”

She posed from different angles and wore her hair in loose curls to complete the look. 

Viewers were amazed by how similar Monika’s look was to those currently on sale in costume shops for Halloween.

Monika said her dress was from women’s fashion boutique Honey and her boots were from Steve Madden.

The video racked up over 107,000 likes, which inspired her to make a part two.

She teamed a black dress that she already owned with thigh-high black boots and wore her hair in two braids for a Wednesday Addams-inspired look. 

Thousands of people liked the post and said it was their “favorite” as they asked her to share more outfit ideas.

“Love this idea,” one person said commenting on the pirate costume.

“Before the scarf, I thought this was Chucky’s bride,” a second chimed in.
